Final notes

The design is responsive and shrinks nicely when the width of the viewport requires so. I made an executive decision to not have layout-changing breakpoints, and only minimal font-size-tweaking because for some reason jQuery shrinks the font size in the tabs.

I tested this component on my rather old iPod touch 4th gen — pretty tiny screen estate when compared to most smartphones — and the result was acceptable. More fiddling could be done, but I'm sticking to the two hours deadline.
